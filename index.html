A Smarter Way To Enjoy Music On Discord

Riya Bot lets you play high-quality YouTube music with simple commands. Just join a voice channel, play a mood or specific song, and let the bot handle the rest. Built with ❤️ for community music sessions.

Add Riya Bot to your server

💜 Easy to Use

No complicated commands. Just type !play, !mood happy, or !join and let Riya handle the music.

🚀 Blazingly Fast

Built with Python and asynchronous handling for minimal delays. Queueing and skipping are instant and smooth.

🎧 Free Forever

No premium paywalls, no ads. All features are 100% free as long as you bring your own YouTube/Spotify links.

🔓 Open Source

Fully open source. Review, fork, or contribute via GitHub. We love transparency and community-driven features.

📜 Commands

  • !join — Join your voice channel
  • !play [url] — Play or queue a song
  • !mood [happy|sad|focus] — Auto-play music by mood
  • !skip — Skip current song
  • !stop — Stop music and clear queue
  • !nowplaying — Show current song
  • !leave — Disconnect the bot
